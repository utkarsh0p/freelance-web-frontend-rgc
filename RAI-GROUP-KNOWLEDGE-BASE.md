## 7. Brand Voice & Design Cues

- **Tone:** aspirational corporate + devotional. Recurring vocabulary: *visionary, divine inspiration, Maa Adishakti, integrity, future-ready, value-driven, trust, excellence, nation building, growth with purpose*.
- **Signature phrases** worth reusing: "growth with purpose, strength with values, and success with gratitude" · "Divine Power • Eternal Prosperity • Supreme Vision" · "Communication is the key".
- **Visual identity from the logo:** black + rich gold, ornate/regal framing, devotional imagery. The current hero uses bright orange/yellow — gold/saffron tones are clearly on-brand (also auspicious in the Hindu context).
- **Audience:** Indian consumers and prospective partners/franchisees; the site is as much a credibility statement for the group as a customer funnel.

## 8. Content Gaps — things to ask the client for before/while building

1. **No email address or physical address** for the group itself anywhere on the site (only WhatsApp). Crushburg's Bengaluru address is the only location signal.
2. **7 of 9 brand domains are dead or stubs** — decide whether the new site should link out, or instead host a proper page per brand (recommended, since most external links currently 404).
3. **No brand exists yet** for Bar & Restaurant, Hotel & Hospitality, or Wellness & Pharmaceutical beyond Herbgiri — confirm whether these are "coming soon" ventures.
4. **Baretha logo is only 156×161 px** — request a high-res version.
5. **No social media links** for the group (Crushburg has @crushburgindia).
6. **Newsletter promises "10% off your first purchase"** but the group site sells nothing — confirm if this copy should be kept.
7. No founding year, registration details (CIN), team page, testimonials, press, or milestones — all typical conglomerate-site sections the client may want.
8. Relationship between "Prithvi Consumer Staples Private Limited" (Crushburg's stated parent) and RAI Group is unclear — worth clarifying for the corporate-structure section.
